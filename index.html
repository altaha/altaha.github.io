Intro

My name is Ahmed. I do Software and Computer Engineering things. Based in Toronto!

I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering. I have also been working on a part-time Masters in Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto. Never stop learning!

After finishing undergrad I worked at Qualcomm, implementing Linux Kernel device drivers that went into some of the most iconic Android devices. My work was primarily of embedded nature and using C. Since 2015, I joined TopHat, an ed-tech startup in Toronto that is a leader in classroom engagement. My role is a full stack web software developer, using Python and Javascript, which has been a big and exciting departure.

I'm excited by technology and the technical aspects of things, but also interested in building products and putting the user first!
